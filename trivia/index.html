Part 2: Free Response

About how many times do giant pandas poop each day?

They poop 45 times per day. After eating anywhere from 26 to 84 pounds of bamboo each day, giant pandas need to get rid of the indigestible parts of the plant. That means pooping more than 45 times a day! All those droppings help biologists who study the animals in the wild. Scientists look for the poop to locate the solitary mammals, since they can be hard to find in the misty mountains of southwestern China where they live.
